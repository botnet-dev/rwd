ROWODO in a Glance
Rondo Women’s Development Organization, popularly known as ROWODO, is a non-profit organization (NGO). ROWODO is a member based organization, female headed organization established in 2011 and registered officially, 3rd February 2014, with a certificate of registration 00NGO/00006923. An organization initiated due to Gender inequalities, inadequate access to Economic opportunities and Reproductive Health education for Women and Youth in Lindi region, a group of women sit at Rondo on 14th August 2013 and agreed to establish an organization so called Rondo women’s development organization for the purpose of reducing afore mentioned challenges for Youth and Women in Lindi region.
ROWODO started its activities with its own resources in the community of a small remote village of Rondo in Lindi District. The type of activities will be undertaken by the organization range from capacity building to service delivery, with focus remaining on the deprived and marginalized segments of the society. Whereas in terms of operations what characterizes ROWODO’s functioning its efforts to (a) enlist involvement of the locals when initiating activities at the grass root level; and (b) mobilize women for bringing peace, tolerance, and development in society. In collaboration with likely minded organization ROWODO shall implement its activities.
ROWODO has country as well global development network, an organization advocating for change and connecting countries to knowledge, experience and resources to assist people build a better life. ROWODO actively participate as member in regular meeting, workshops and seminars; exchange views and ideas, share information and best practices; observe national and international days of action together; review priority issue and recommend practical and realistic solution to problems.
ROWODO has a group of well-experienced, skilled and professional core staff having training and exposure in the head office and branch offices. In addition to its skill personnel’s a group of experts attached with ROWODO who provide necessary consultancy including guideline, policy making etc.

Our Values

Our Vision

Having society with a good prosperity and equal rights for all social classes as well as poverty reduction.

Our Mission

To develop and enhance society economically, to advocate rights of youth, children and women group: such as provision of legal aids, reproductive health education to youth in school, HIV/AIDS education, environmental conservation, Human rights and Nutrition.

Achievements
Remarkable ROWODO Achievements
  • The organization has participated in commemoration of World Breast Feeding week sponsored by Save the Children International in Lindi and Ruangwa districts in 2015 national campaign.
  • Cooperating with partners including government and other NGOs in various events.
  • Organization has successes in mobilizing communities on environment conservation and friendly farming system.
  • Participated in mobilizing in “Binti Lindi campaign” with the purpose of influencing and motivating girls to put efforts in studies.
  • Have mobilized community on HIV AIDS education.
